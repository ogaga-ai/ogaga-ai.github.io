Enzyme Thermostability Prediction with ESM-2

PyTorch HuggingFace Transformers ESM-2 (150M) scikit-learn Ridge Regression Gradient Boosting UMAP NumPy pandas matplotlib

The ML model predicts enzyme melting temperature (Tm) from amino acid sequence using Meta's ESM-2 protein language model trained on 250M sequences across all of evolution. ESM-2 mean-pooled embeddings augmented with physicochemical features achieve Spearman ρ = 0.673, a 20% improvement over the hand-crafted baseline. Directly motivated by industrial enzyme engineering for fermentation and bioconversion systems.
